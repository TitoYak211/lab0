Code-254 is a free intensive one week summer program in Nairobi, Kenya that introduces high schoolers to programming and algorithms.

Our Mission Read more

If you can think it — you can code it

Mission

Code-254 will promote holistic learning for all the deserving students that will have been selected on the based on academic ability and character, and become the leader in Kenya, Africa and beyond, in the promotion of technology.

Vision

We envision a Kenya where access to programming and principles of Computer Science is the norm for all children to be desciples for change in their communities.

Goals

Code-254 is targeting to reach 10,000 students in 10 years. Majority of the learners will be selected from public schools which are heavily underfunded and have limited to no exposure to Computer Science and art of programming.

Achievements

This is a project in progress. We hope to launch in 2020 and start fundraising to admission our first class in the summer of 2020.
